The output files of DLC 1.5.
The wind files are provided.


- The wind fields used were 150s long.
    - 50s was steady state wind with no shear.
    - 100s Extreme Shear Wind (following equations 26 & 27 in IEC61400-01 standard)


- The naming of the outputfiles is following this:
     - Type1: Positive vertical shear
     - Type2: Negative vertical shear
     - Type3: Positive horizontal shear
     - Type4: Negative horizontal shear
